Greatest Small Forwards of All Time
Greatest Small Forwards of All Time




  1. LeBron James

    Accolades: 4x NBA Champion, 4x Finals MVP, 4x MVP, 17x All Star, 16x All NBA

    Bio: LeBron James is the only player in NBA history to lead 5 different head coaches to the NBA finals.

  2. Larry Bird

    Accolades: 3x NBA Champion, 2x Finals MVP, 3x MVP, 12x All Star, 10x All NBA

    Bio: Larry Bird is the only player in NBA history to win an MVP and the Coach of the Year award.

  3. Kevin Durant

    Accolades: 2x NBA Champion, 2x Finals MVP, 2014 MVP, 11x All Star, 9x All NBA

    Bio: Kevin Durant is commonly considered the most complete scorer in NBA history, having 4 scoring titles.

  4. Julius Erving

    Accolades: 3x NBA/ABA Champion, 2x ABA Playoff MVP, 16x All Star, 12x All NBA/ABA

    Bio: Dr J. is known for his big afro and high flying dunks.

  5. John Havlicek

    Accolades: 8x NBA Champion, 1974 Finals MVP, 13x All Star, 11x All NBA

    Bio: John Havlicek was the side kick to Bill Russell, and in the 70s helped the Celtics continue their success, without their franchise's greatest player.
