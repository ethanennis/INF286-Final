Greatest Shooting Guards of All Time




  1. Michael Jordan

    Accolades: 6x NBA Champion, 6x Finals MVP, 5x MVP, 14x All Star, 11x All NBA

    Bio: Michael Jordan is commonly refered to as the greatest basketball player of all time, and is credited with help globablize the sport of basketball as a member of the 1992 "Dream Team"

  2. Kobe Bryant

    Accolades: 5x NBA Champion, 2x Finals MVP, 2008 MVP, 18x All Star, 15x All NBA

    Bio: Kobe Bryant helped lead the Los Angeles Lakers to 5 NBA titles during the 2000s, Bryant also won an Oscar after playing for his short film.

  3. Jerry West

    Accolades: 1972 NBA Champion, 1969 Finals MVP, 14x All Star, 12x All NBA

    Bio: Jerry West is the NBA's logo, and is known also as being arguably the greatest executive in NBA history, thanks to decisions such as, Drafting Magic Johnson or Kobe Bryant.

  4. Dwyane Wade

    Accolades: 3x NBA Champion, 2006 Finals MVP, 13x All Star, 8x All NBA

    Bio: Dwyane Wade led the Miami Heat to the 2006 NBA Championship with one of the greatest finals performances of all time against the Mavericks.

  5. James Harden

    Accolades: 2018 MVP, 9x All Star, 7x All NBA

    Bio: James Harden led the Houston Rockets in 2018 only one win shy of defeating the Golden State Warriors, a team commonly considered unbeatable at the time.
