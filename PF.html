Greatest Power Forwards of All Time






  1. Tim Duncan

    Accolades: 5x NBA Champion, 3x Finals MVP, 2x MVP, 15x All Star, 15x All NBA

    Bio: Tim Duncan was born in the US Virgin Islands and grew up wanting to be a swimmer.

  2. Bob Pettit

    Accolades: 1958 NBA Champion, 2x MVP, 11x All Star, 11x All NBA

    Bio: Bob Pettit won the NBA's first MVP, and was a part of the only team to defeat Russell's Celtics in the NBA Finals.

  3. Dirk Nowitzki

    Accolades: 2011 NBA Champion, 2011 Finals MVP, 2007 MVP, 14x All Star, 12x All NBA

    Bio: Dirk Nowizki was the first German NBA All Star and helped lead Dallas to their first NBA Championship.

  4. Dolph Schayes

    Accolades: 1955 NBA Champion, 12x All Star, 12x All NBA

    Bio: Dolph Schayes led the Syracause Nationals to an NBA Championship and played his entire career there until his final year where the moved to Philidelphia where they became the 76ers.

  5. Kevin Garnett

    Accolades: 2008 NBA Champion, 2004 MVP, 15x All Star, 9x All NBA

    Bio: Kevin Garnett is one of five players to have won MVP and DPOY.
