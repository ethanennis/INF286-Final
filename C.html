Greatest Centers of All Time
Greatest Centers of All Time




  1. Bill Russell

    Accolades: 11x NBA Champion, 5x MVP, 12x All Star, 11x All NBA

    Bio: Bill Russell holds the record for the most championships, and was the first black coach in american sports.

  2. Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

    Accolades: 6x NBA Champion, 6x MVP, 19x All Star, 15x All NBA

    Bio: Kareem Abdul-Jabbar is commonly referred to as the greatest college basketball player and has an argument as the greatest professional basketball player of all time with a record 6 MVPs.

  3. Wilt Chamberlain

    Accolades: 2x NBA Champion, 1972 Finals MVP, 13x All Star, 10x All NBA

    Bio: Wilt Chamberlain once averaged 50 points and 25 rebounds per game for an entire season, he also scored 100 points in one game that season.

  4. Shaquille O'Neal

    Accolades: 4x NBA Champion, 3x Finals MVP, 2000 MVP, 15x All Star, 14x All NBA

    Bio: Shaquille O'Neal has broken more backboards than made 3 pointers.

  5. George Mikan

    Accolades: 5x BAA/NBA Champion, 4x All Star, 5x All BAA/NBA

    Bio: George Mikan led the Minneapolis Lakers to the NBA's first dynasty in the 1950s.
